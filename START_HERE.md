## 🔗 Connect Frontend to Backend

After Render gives you a URL:

1. Visit: https://vercel.com/victorvengateshs-projects/frontend/settings/environment-variables

2. Edit `VITE_API_BASE_URL`:
   - New value: Your Render backend URL
   - Save

3. Redeploy:
   - Go to: https://vercel.com/victorvengateshs-projects/frontend
   - Click "Deployments" → "..." → "Redeploy"
